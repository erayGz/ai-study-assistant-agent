Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science that works on making systems that can do things that need human-like intelligence. These tasks could be figuring things out, learning, searching, planning, and understanding language.

An intelligent agent is a piece of software that takes in information from its surroundings, processes it, and then does things to reach a goal.


Testing is a necessary step in making software. Unit testing looks at single parts or functions. Integration testing makes sure that different parts of the system work together as they should.

Deployment is the process of getting a software system ready for other people to install, set up, and use.
